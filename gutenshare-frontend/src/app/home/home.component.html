About the gutenshare network

The gutenshare network is a web platform independent from lecturers and educational institutions, for students by students, to share study related resources. You upload documents you are willing to share with your peers.

Besides the search functionality, you will be able to easily locate contributions of others through the gutenshare document browser.

As means of quality control you can rate documents and discuss them in the comment section.
